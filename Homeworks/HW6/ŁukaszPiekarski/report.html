B) SHAP Variable Importance¶

Differences¶

  1. PVI and Gini impurity have different variable rankings. SHAP and PVI have a similar variable ranking but with some differences in the order.
  2. The absolute values of importance differ between the methods. Gini impurity tends to give higher importance values compared to PVI and SHAP.

Why are they different¶

  1. PVI focuses on the impact of dropping variables individually. Gini impurity measures impurity reduction during tree construction. SHAP values consider the average contribution of each feature to the model output.
  2. PVI and Gini impurity may not capture interactions between features. SHAP values explicitly consider interactions and dependencies between features.
